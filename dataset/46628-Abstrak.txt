ABSTRACT  
  
            Background. The development of technology and information has progressed very 
rapidly from year to year. One of them is development gadget  which is increasingly 
widespread, almost all individuals, both children and adults, now have it gadget 
orsmartphone . Objective. This research was conducted to determine the effect of 
usegadget  on the formation of character in early childhood at Yabis Paud Bontang 
city.Method. The method used in this study is an analytic survey method with an 
approach Cross sectional study with 57 respondents. Results. The research results have a 
distribution of students who have use gadget high 30 (52.6%) and showed the formation 
of good character as many as 43 (75.4%) students, while the use gadget low by 27 
(47.4%) students and poor character formation by 14 (24.6) students. Once done Chi-
Square obtained p-value of 0.399 (p <0.05). Conclusion. This shows that t here is no effect 
of use gadget  on the formation of character in early childhood at Yabis Paud Bontang 
city.
