 ABSTRAK  
MULTAZAM MUSTADJAB. Pengembangan Jalur Pejalan Kaki Berbasis 
Urban Network Analysis di Jalan Sultan Alauddin, Kecamatan Rappocini  
(dibimbing oleh Nini Apriani Rumata dan M. Nurhidayat).  
 
Meningkatnya pertumbuhan penduduk meni mbulkan tantangan dalam 
mobilitas serta penyediaan fasilitas transportasi berkelanjutan. Berjalan 
kaki merupakan moda transportasi ramah lingkungan yang mendukung 
mobilitas perkotaan. Namun, rendahnya kualitas jalur pejalan kaki di 
kawasan perkotaan berdam pak pada menurunnya kenyamanan, 
keamanan, dan aksesibilitas bagi pejalan kaki. Penelitian ini bertujuan untuk 
menganalisis kondisi eksisting jalur pejalan kaki, mengukur tingkat 
keterhubungan jaringan jalan terhadap populasi penduduk melalui metrik 
Between ness Centrality,  menilai persepsi masyarakat terhadap tingkat 
walkability, serta merumuskan arahan pengembangan jalur pejalan kaki 
sesuai standar teknis. Metode penelitian menggunakan pendekatan Mix 
Method,  yaitu analisis kuantitatif melalui Urban Network Analysis  (UNA) 
berbasis ArcGIS serta survei kualitatif dengan kuesioner Walkability Index  
kepada pengguna jalur pejalan kaki. Hasil penelitian menunjukkan bahwa 
Segmen 3 (Jl. Tallasalapang –Jl. Syech Yusuf) memiliki nilai Betweenness 
Centrality tertinggi, m enandakan beban pergerakan paling dominan dan 
menjadikannya prioritas pengembangan. Sementara itu, hasil perhitungan 
Walkability Index  sebesar 25,5 berada pada kategori Not Walkable,  dengan 
skor terendah pada infrastruktur disabilitas, ketersediaan jalur, dan fasilitas 
pendukung. Kondisi ini menunjukkan masih tingginya potensi konflik antara 
pejalan kaki dengan kendaraan. Penelitian menyimpulkan perlunya 
pembangunan trotoar dengan lebar minimal 2 meter, penyediaan 
penyeberangan yang aman, penertiban aktivit as pengganggu, 
penambahan fasilitas pendukung, serta integrasi halte dengan jalur pejalan 
kaki. Implementasi strategi ini diharapkan mampu meningkatkan 
kenyamanan, keselamatan, dan keberlanjutan jalur pejalan kaki di Jalan 
Sultan Alauddin, Kecamatan Rappoc ini. 
 
Kata kunci:  Urban Network Analysis, Betweenness Centrality, Walkability,  
Jalur Pejalan Kaki, Jalan Sultan Alauddin  Kecamatan Rappocini.

 ABSTRACT  
MULTAZAM MUSTADJAB. Development of Pedestrian Pathways Based 
on Urban Network Analysis on Sultan Alauddin Street, Rappocini District  
(supervised  by Nini Apriani Rumata and M. Nurhidayat).  
 
The rapid growth of urban population has created challenges in mobility and 
the provision of sustainable transportation facilitie s. Walking is considered 
the most environmentally friendly mode of sustainable transportation that 
supports urban mobility. However, the poor quality of pedestrian pathways 
in urban areas reduces comfort, safety, and accessibility for pedestrians. 
This stu dy aims to analyze the existing condition of pedestrian pathways, 
measure the connectivity of the street network to the population using the 
Betweenness Centrality metric, assess public perceptions of walkability, 
and formulate development strategies accor ding to technical standards. 
The research employed a mixed -method approach, combining quantitative 
spatial analysis using Urban Network Analysis (UNA) with ArcGIS software 
and qualitative surveys through the Walkability Index questionnaire 
distributed to p edestrian users. The results show that Segment 3 ( Jl. 
Tallasalapang –Jl. Syech Yusuf) has the highest Betweenness Centrality 
value, indicating the most dominant pedestrian movement load and making 
it the main priority for development. Meanwhile, the Walkability Index score 
of 25.5 falls into the Not Walkable category, with the lowest scores in 
disability infrastructure, pathway availability, and supporting facilities. These 
conditions highlight the high potential for conflicts between pedestrians and 
motorized vehicles. The study concludes that improvements should include 
the construction of standard sidewalks with a minimum width of 2 meters, 
the provision of safe crossings, regulation of disruptive activities, the 
addition of supporting facilities, a nd the integration of bus stops with 
pedestrian pathways. The implementation of these strategies is expected to 
improve comfort, safety, and the sustainability of pedestrian pathways along 
Sultan Alauddin Street, Rappocini District.  
 
Keywords:  Urban Networ k Analysis, Betweenness Centrality, Walkability, 
Pedestrian Pathways, Sultan Alauddin Street Rappocini District.
